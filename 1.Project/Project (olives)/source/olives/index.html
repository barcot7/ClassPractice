Course Outline:

Session One: Introduction/Getting Started [+]

  • Brainstorming Book Ideas
  • A simple yet dynamic technique you can use to generate more story ideas
  • How to choose a Best-Seller Book Title
  • How to determine which type of story you will like to write
  • How to find time to write in between school and extra curricular activities
  • The power of pre-writing

Session Two: Beginnings, Middles, and Ends [+]

  • How to choose a theme that is relevant to your target audience
  • How to select an age group to write for…and what they really want!
  • Choosing and developing your setting
  • Identifying key factors for developing credible characters
  • How to develop an effective beginning, middle, and end to your picture book

Session Three: Testing out your story idea/Preparing for Publication [+]

  • Ways to evaluate your story idea and get an unbiased, honest read-through
  • Common mistakes most beginner writers make
  • Pros and cons of self publishing vs. traditional publishing
  • Ways to fund your book project
  • Tips from other successful child authors

Session Four: Parent Edition [+]

Jeremiah and Joshua’s parents, Norman and Olivia West will host an EXCLUSIVE webinar: “Written Success: How Parents Can Help Their Child Authors Achieve Their Dreams.” Norman and Olivia will discuss some of the joys and pains of promoting your kids and how to avoid the mistakes they have made with Jeremiah and Joshua to help your child achieve rapid success as an author! Parents, you will not want to miss this!
